📘 GrowGuru Dynamic Active Stocks

Systematic momentum strategy using F&O stocks with defensive allocation and regime based risk management.

80% Stocks 20% Defensive Quarterly Rebalance

🎯 Strategy Objective

Capture strong momentum stocks while maintaining a defensive buffer to reduce drawdowns during weak market phases.

🌐 Investment Universe

Parameter Rule
Universe F&O Stocks
Approx Size ~200 Stocks
Reason Liquidity and institutional participation

🏗 Portfolio Structure

Basket Allocation
Equity Momentum Basket 80%
Defensive Basket 20%

Defensive assets include Liquidcase and Goldcase.

📈 Momentum Model

Timeframe Weight
1 Year Return 50%
6 Month Return 30%
3 Month Return 20%
Momentum Score = (1Y × 50%) + (6M × 30%) + (3M × 20%)

🏆 Stock Selection

  • Select top 10 stocks based on momentum score
  • Allocate equally across selected stocks
  • Equity allocation = 80% of portfolio

🔄 Rebalancing

Portfolio review frequency is quarterly.

  • Recalculate momentum ranks
  • Exit stocks below rank threshold
  • Replace with next highest ranked stocks

🚪 Exit Rule

Condition Action
Rank ≤ 20 Hold
Rank > 20 Exit and Replace

🛡 Regime Control

If market regime turns defensive, reduce equity exposure.

Asset Allocation
Stocks 40%
Defensive Assets 60%

🔁 Recovery Rule

When regime normalizes, revert back to the base allocation.

Asset Allocation
Stocks 80%
Defensive Assets 20%
